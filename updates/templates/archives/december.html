December update

It's almost time for the holiday break, so we'd like to update you with the exciting happenings at Microcosm.app over the last two months.

UX research and visual refresh

Firstly, a huge thank-you to all who took part in our user interviews and feedback sessions. Your help has been invaluable in refining what we're building.

Toward the end of October we took what we'd learned over the past year and combined it with more UX research to produce a hugely improved web experience. This is almost completed and we're currently incorporating the new visual design into the existing project.

We're excited to be able to reveal all of this work with a public launch early next year.

New features

While the new design work has been happening, we've also made great progress towards our targets for public launch. These include:

  • Search capability deployed to all sites on Microcosm.app
  • A brand new way of seeing streaming updates on forums you take part in
  • Rich content embedding (so if you post a link to e.g. YouTube, we can embed the video)
  • Roll-out of private messaging (huddles) to all sites
  • Tools for site owners to create and manage their sites.

As ever, do join us on our discussion site meta.microcosm.app if you'd like to find out more or have any suggestions. We're always around to answer questions.

The next time we email will be our public launch (early next year), which will enable anyone to set up a site on Microcosm.app. In the meantime, if you're thinking of setting up a community, email us directly at founders@microcosm.cc and we'll get you set up right away.

Cheers,

David and Matt
